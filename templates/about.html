Social Media

XPLR.IO is a Social Media platform that lets users create content about their travel experiances as well as browse/swipe/look-up posts to get insigts about unique destinations.

Coordinates

We urge users to share the coordinates of the locations that they post about so that the people who view their content can not only get inspired, but actually go to that location and experiance the beauty for themselves.

Search/Feed

Our feed is swipe based on Mobile(think Tinder), and scroll based on Web. We use Machine Learning to give you more of what you want to see, but also let you filter and search for posts manually
